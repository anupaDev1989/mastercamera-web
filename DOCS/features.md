# ✨ Stackr Camera Feature Highlights

Below is a benefits-focused list of Stackr Camera's core features. Each feature clearly states what it does and the value it provides to photographers, scientists, documentarians, field teams, and hobbyists.

---

## 1. Fast, Professional Capture
**Capture like a professional**  
Grid overlays, compass guides, watermarks, voice notes, and live metadata. Everything you need for professional captures, built right into the camera view.

- **Live grid overlays** and guides for precise framing  
- **Watermark logos** on every shot  
- Add **voice notes** at capture time  
- **Metadata fields** baked into every photo  
- Record **video with automatic watermarking**

---

## 2. Organize From Capture
**Structure from the moment you shoot**  
No more photo chaos. Organize into projects, tag automatically, add custom metadata fields tailored to your workflow—all while you're still on location.

- Create **projects/stacks** for any job or initiative  
- **Tag photos** at capture or in bulk later  
- **Custom metadata fields** for your specific workflow (client name, location code, etc.)  
- Metadata is **searchable and filterable**

---

## 3. Find Anything Instantly
**Search with precision**  
Locate photos by date, location (GPS), tags, custom fields, or notes. Never lose track of a shot again.

- Search by **date range** and specific dates  
- Find photos by **location on an interactive map**  
- Filter by **tags, custom fields, and metadata**  
- Search by **voice notes and text annotations**  
- See all results on a **map** if they're geotagged

---

## 4. Markup & Edit On Device
**Edit without leaving the app**  
Crop, rotate, adjust, and annotate. Mark up photos with drawings, text, and shapes. Everything happens locally, instantly.

- **Crop, rotate**, adjust brightness and contrast  
- **Draw, annotate**, add text and shapes  
- **Undo/redo** with full editor history  
- Create **multiple edits** without modifying the original

---

## 5. Completely Offline
**Works everywhere, no internet required**  
Bad connectivity? No problem. Stackr Camera works with full functionality offline. Capture, organize, search, and edit without a signal.

- **Full app functionality** without internet  
- No mandatory **cloud syncing** in v1  
- Works on **flights, remote locations, and off-grid shoots**  
- **Sync later** when you have connectivity (v2)

---

## 6. Your Data Stays Yours
**Complete privacy by design**  
All your photos and data live locally on your device. No cloud uploads, no tracking, no analytics. Your work is yours alone.

- **Zero cloud uploads** in v1 (local storage only)  
- No **telemetry or user tracking**  
- No mandatory **login or account creation**  
- Your **privacy policy** is in the app; read it anytime  
- **Full control** over where your files go
